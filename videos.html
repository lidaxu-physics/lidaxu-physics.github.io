Science communication

Physics, in Mandarin.

I make a Mandarin physics-history video series on Bilibili. It's where my outreach side lives — when the lab work is done and I want to tell stories instead of write papers.

Bilibili videos are in Mandarin Chinese

The current series is a five-part deep dive into Paul Dirac — strange genius, inventor of antimatter, accidental founder of quantum field theory — based mostly on his biography The Strangest Man. The original Chinese episode titles are kept as-is below; English subtitles give a sense of each one.
